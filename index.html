Spectrum Insurance Brokerage Services, Inc. is a multi-faceted insurance brokerage and risk management services firm. Our experienced staff enables us to handle all lines of casualty, property, and bond insurance, along with claims administration. In addition, we also provide employee benefits programs.
As a full service organization we are dedicated to providing services and professionalism to our clients, with a commitment to a comprehensive and affordable insurance program. We have access to both admitted and specialty lines companies. This enables our firm to react immediately to the change in the insurance industry that could have an impact on our client's insurance programs.
